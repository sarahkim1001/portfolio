CafeDVD

An e-commerce platform designed with UX research, completed by users’ cultural contributions to media democracy, using iterative design and multimedia cohesion to enhance engagement and community impact.

CafeDVD is a platform for renting indie film DVDs, emphasizing accessibility to niche and hard-to-find titles. Through user-centered design processes, including surveys and iterative prototyping, the platform was developed to foster a community-driven experience. The interface integrates multimedia elements—trailers, user reviews, and curated playlists—to create an engaging, accessible experience. The purpose is to democratize access to independent cinema, empowering users to contribute to a shared cultural archive, inspired by principles of media equity and collaborative storytelling.
